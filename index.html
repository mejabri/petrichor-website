Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Petrichor.ai ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our mobile applications handle your data.

Data We Collect

Our games collect minimal data to function:

  • Game progress: High scores and settings are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or transmit this data to our servers.
  • Leaderboards: If you sign in to Game Center (iOS) or Google Play Games (Android), your scores are submitted to their respective services, governed by Apple's and Google's privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

Our apps use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: Serves ads within the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Game Center / Google Play Games: Used for leaderboards. See Apple's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our games are designed for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ad content is configured to comply with applicable regulations.

Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store personal data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at petrichor.contact@gmail.com.
